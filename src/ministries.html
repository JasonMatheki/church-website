Our Ministries

Children's Ministry

Our Children's Ministry provides a safe and fun environment for kids to learn about Jesus. We offer Sunday school classes and special events throughout the year.

Youth Ministry

The Youth Ministry is dedicated to helping teenagers grow in their faith through Bible studies, youth group meetings, and mission trips. We aim to create a supportive community for young believers.

Women's Ministry

Our Women's Ministry encourages women to connect with each other and deepen their relationship with God. We offer Bible studies, prayer groups, and fellowship events designed specifically for women.

Men's Ministry

The Men's Ministry aims to build strong, godly men through regular meetings, Bible studies, and service projects. We focus on discipleship and mentorship to help men grow in their faith.

Community Outreach

Our Community Outreach programs seek to serve those in need within our community. We organize food drives, clothing donations, and volunteer opportunities to make a positive impact on our neighbors.
